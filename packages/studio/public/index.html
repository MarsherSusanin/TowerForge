Welcome to TowerForge Editor

Design a complete tower-defense game from data — then play it instantly. Here's the loop:

1 · Author
Edit Enemies, Towers, Waves and Missions in the sidebar. Sliders, level ladders and pickers — no JSON.
2 · Playtest
Open Playtest to drop towers and run waves live on your unsaved project.
3 · Tune
Sim charts the difficulty curve; the top-bar pill flags broken references as you type.
4 · Ship
Build Targets emits a playable web bundle. Enable MCP in Settings to let AI agents help.
